| **Gamma**                            | $X \sim \text{Gamma}(\alpha, \beta)$     | $f(x) = \frac{\beta^\alpha}{\Gamma(\alpha)} x^{\alpha - 1} e^{-\beta x}$     | $L(\beta) \propto \beta^{n\alpha} e^{-\beta \sum x_i}$                                        | $\beta \sim \text{Gamma}(a, b)$                                               | $\beta \mid x \sim \text{Gamma}(a + n\alpha, b + \sum x_i)$                                                        | $\frac{a}{b}$                                 | $\frac{a}{b^2}$                                                 |

| **Beta**                             | $X \sim \text{Beta}(\alpha, \beta)$      | $f(x) = \frac{x^{\alpha - 1}(1 - x)^{\beta - 1}}{B(\alpha, \beta)}$          | Rarely used in likelihood settings                                                            | Rarely used as likelihood                                                     | No standard conjugate prior                                                                                        | $\frac{\alpha}{\alpha + \beta}$               | $\frac{\alpha\beta}{(\alpha + \beta)^2(\alpha + \beta + 1)}$    |
